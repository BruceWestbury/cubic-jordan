# cubic-graphs pipeline

## Stage 1
Generate closed graphs.

F4:
- closed trivalent graphs

E6:
- closed trivalent bipartite graphs

## Stage 2
Generate sources/sites with provenance.

## Stage 3
Compute closed graph evaluations.

## Stage 4
Generate local relations/witnesses.

## Stage 5
Compute witness caches.

## Important
- Evaluations are mathematical values.
- Witnesses explain computations.
- Pentagon/hexagon reductions are witness methods, not the definition of evaluation.
- Do not infer missing mathematics.
